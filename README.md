# Antigravity Menu

The ultimate control center for your Antigravity Kit.

## Features

- **Antigravity Hub**: Access all tools from the StatusBar.
- **Three-Level Menu**: Organized by Agents, Skills, and Workflows.
- **Auto-Discovery**: Automatically finds and categorizes your custom Skills from `.agent/skills`.
- **Customizable**: Edit `.agent/ag-menu-structure.json` to organize folders your way.

## Usage

1. Click **Antigravity Hub** in the Status Bar (bottom right).
2. Browse or search for an Agent/Skill.
3. Click to **Copy** the activation command.
4. Paste (`Ctrl+V`) into your chat window.
